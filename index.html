About

Decoding the language
of proteins

I am an undergraduate biotechnology student at IIT Hyderabad, specializing in computational approaches to protein design, antibody engineering and molecular dynamic simulations. My research bridges wet-lab molecular biology with cutting-edge machine learning — building models that can read, reason about, and ultimately design biological sequences.

My current focus is on diffusion models for protein design, heavy-light chain pairing in antibodies, and designing topological DNA structures for MD simulations.

Beyond computation, I am deeply interested in the philosophical dimensions of synthetic biology — what it means to engineer life, and the responsibilities that come with it.
